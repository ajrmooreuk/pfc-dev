**AI STRATEGY PROGRAMME PORTFOLIO**

Phase 2: Delivery Programme

*February \- May 2026*

# **Executive Summary**

This programme portfolio outlines the AI Strategy delivery initiatives planned for Phase 2 (February \- May 2026). The programme encompasses five parallel workstreams: Infrastructure Projects, Business Projects, Communications, Resource/Training, and Governance. Four key milestones mark progressive delivery of AI-enabled capabilities across the organisation.

# **Programme Timeline Overview**

The 4-month delivery programme is structured around monthly milestone gates, with activities progressing from infrastructure foundation through to full operational capability with measurable AI-driven benefits.

| Milestone | Target Date | Success Criteria |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Milestone 1** | End February 2026 | Infrastructure planned & resources selected |
| **Milestone 2** | End March 2026 | Initial business projects are live |
| **Milestone 3** | End April 2026 | All business projects are live |
| **Milestone 4** | End May 2026 | Core processes (prospect, upsell, renew) delivering material AI benefits |

# **Workstream 1: Infrastructure Projects**

Technical foundation and platform development enabling AI capabilities across the organisation.

## **February Deliverables**

* Markets View: Market intelligence and competitive analysis platform

## **May Deliverables**

* API Filter (Perudio): External API integration and filtering capabilities
