## Strategic Framework

The AI Strategy Programme follows the VSOM (Vision-Strategy-Objectives-Metrics) framework integrated with BSC (Balanced Scorecard) perspectives:

- **5 Strategic Pillars:** Client Excellence (25%), Sales & Growth (20%), Operational Efficiency (15%), Knowledge & Advisory (15%), Data Foundation (25%)
- **RCSG Governance:** Cross-cutting Risk, Compliance, Security & Governance layer
- **4 BSC Perspectives:** Financial (F1-F4), Customer (C1-C4), Internal Process (P1-P4), Learning & Growth (L1-L4)
- **16 Strategic Objectives** with OKR-aligned key results and 24-month targets
- **50+ Initiatives** across 12 capability domains
- **4 Cross-Cutting Themes:** Data-Driven Decisions, Intelligent Automation, Workforce Augmentation, Compliance by Design

## Document Evolution

This document set originated from the AI Strategy Steering Meeting (November 2025) as simple initiative summaries. It has been elaborated through several iterations into a comprehensive enterprise strategy framework:

2. **v2.0** (Feb 2026) — Extended with strategic pillars, themes, and core capabilities
3. **v3.0** (Feb 2026) — Added BSC strategy map, 16 objectives, OKR alignment, initiative mapping
4. **v3.1** (Feb 2026) — Added RCSG Governance layer, completed all 16 BSC objectives in visual maps, enterprise perspective with EA audit workstream alignment, Wave 1 priorities
